What your BMI result means

A BMI range of 18.5 to 24.9 is considered a 'healthy weight'. Maintaining a healthy weight may lower your chances of experiencng health issues later on, such as obesity and type II diabetes. Aim for a nutritious diet with reduced fat and sugar content, incorporating amplae fruits and vegetables.Additionally, strive for regular physical activity, ideally about 30 minutes daily, about five days a week.
